Honest Answers

Things people often wonder.

If you're considering FaithOn, you probably have one of these on your mind. Here's what we'd want a friend to know.

Is FaithOn a real person?
FaithOn is a thoughtful spiritual companion you reach by text. Real people — pastors, theologians, and writers — shape its voice. Real scripture grounds every word. Think of it like a thoughtful friend who is always available and always rooted in the Bible.
Is FaithOn based on the Bible?
Yes — every devotional, prayer, and reflection draws from Scripture. Verses are cited when shared. You're always welcome to ask for the passage behind a thought.
Can I use FaithOn anytime?
Yes. Day or night, holiday or Sunday, FaithOn responds in seconds. There is no sabbath on which we close.
Do I need an app?
No. FaithOn is a text-message service. If you can send a text, you can use it. No downloads, accounts, or passwords required.
Are conversations private?
Yes. Your conversations are private. We do not sell or share what you write. You can request that everything be permanently deleted at any time.
Can FaithOn pray with me?
Yes — that's one of the most common requests. Ask for a prayer for peace, courage, healing, gratitude, or any specific situation, and FaithOn will write one with you to read together.
Can FaithOn help me during difficult moments?
Yes. Anxiety, grief, fear, loneliness, doubt — these are exactly the moments FaithOn is built for. We respond with care, not clichés.
Does FaithOn replace church or counseling?
No, and we'd never want to. FaithOn is a companion alongside your community, your pastor, and — when needed — your therapist or doctor. In a true emergency, we'll point you to the right kind of help right away.
